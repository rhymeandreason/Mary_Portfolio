I've been designing engaging user experiences for over 10 years, mainly for innovative consumer apps.

My specialty is prototyping interactions in mid to high fidelity. There are many ideas that can only be expressed by trying it out. Getting the details right matters.

While technology is always an inspiration, as a designer I'm often the advocate of the human side of the experience. I strive for mindfulness about accessibility, usability, and watching out for our own biases as technology builders. I love working with a collaborative team that strives for a high bar of creative and technical craft.

I live in Marin, which is close enough to SF and far enough to be out of the bubble.
